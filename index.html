Choose Your Path

The journey ahead is long and treacherous. What path will you choose to guide your actions?

Warrior

Ironclad and onward-bound, inside you without a doubt beats the heart of a fighter! If you choose the warrior path, you'll have to fight to survive with a legendary hero's blade and a sense of adventure! But be careful, because it's dangerous to go alone.

Sorcerer

Fine-tuned with the energy of the universe, your clever demeanor ensures you can find your way out of any tight situation! If you choose the sorcerer path, you'll be equipped with unimaginable cosmic power! But remember, with great power comes great responsibility.

Guardian

Strong, dependable and with a mind of steel, you'd do just about anything to protect those you trust. If you choose the path of a guardian, you'll gain an impenitrable shield and an iron will, ensuring that you'll be capable of defending you and your friends from any threat.
